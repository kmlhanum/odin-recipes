Gado-Gado Recipes

About Gado-Gado

Gado-Gado is a typical Indonesian salad consisting of blanched or steamed vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and boiled eggs, all smothered in a flavorful peanut sauce.

Main Ingredients

  • 200 grams potatoes, boiled and diced
  • 150 grams long beans, cut and blanched/boiled
  • 100 grams bean sprouts, briefly blanched
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 100 grams tofu, fried and diced
  • 100 grams tempeh, fried and diced
  • 100 grams cabbage, thinly sliced and briefly blanched
  • 100 gram cucumber, thinly sliced

Peanut Sauce

  • 200 grams roasted peanuts, fried and ground until smooth
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 red chili peppers (or adjust to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons palm sugar, shaved
  • teaspoon salt
  • 200 ml warm water
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind juice

How to Make it

  1. Prepare and dice all the boiled vegetables.
  2. To make the peanut sauce, grind the garlic and red chilies until smooth. Mix with the ground peanuts.
  3. TAdd the palm sugar, salt, warm water, and tamarind juice to the peanut mixture. Stir until well combined, and the peanut sauce is ready to use.
  4. Arrange the boiled vegetables, tofu, tempeh, and eggs on a serving plate.
  5. Pour a sufficient amount of peanut sauce over the vegetables.
  6. Gado-Gado is ready to be served. Enjoy with crackers or rice cake (lontong) if desired.
